Nevidio Canyon

Explore Nevidio

3+ 4h 1.7km 100€

Nevidio Canyon is one of the most beautiful and most attractive canyons in Montenegro.

It is a part of the Mala Komarnica river, which rises at the foot of the southern slopes of Durmitor, more precisely in Dobro Dol. Plunging, it passes between the peaks of Boljski gred (2091 m.a.s.l.) and Lojanik (2091 m.a.s.l.) to reappear in a gentle valley as a permanent river. Mala Komarnica near the village of Duži merges with Pridvorica and creates Velika Komarnica, which continues through the canyon until entering the artificial Pivsko lake created by the construction of the Mratinje hydroelectric power plant. Below Mratinje Piva flows another 10 km to the confluence with Tara, where together they form the Drina river.

Nevidio is about 2 km long. The entrance is located at 935 m, and the exit at 810 m above sea level.
Secluded and hidden in inaccessible terrain, it aroused attention and curiosity, but there were few organized attempts to get to know and explore it. The first serious attempts were made in 1957. and 1964, which were unsuccessful. There were many strange and fantastic stories about the last unconquered canyon in Europe.
It was like that until August 1965. when members of the P.S.D. Javorak from Nikšić, although with meager equipment, passed the canyon and was the first to present to the world a long-hidden, wild and astonishing beauty.

The first contact with Nevidio is overshadowed by an indescribable beauty, unusual and strange to which a person is not used to. Most of the canyon is in an eternal shadow. Because of the vertical rocks, the sun's rays cannot reach the bottom. On the steep and inaccessible rocks, there are harsh conditions for vegetation, so that the canyon can be said to be a jewel of harsh nature that is both heaven and hell. The width of the canyon in some places is less than one meter. The length is 1700 meters and the crossing takes about 2.5 hours.

PROGRAMME

• Gathering in front of Eco village Nevidio in the morning
• Getting to know the equipment and the type of planned activities.
• Canyoning - descent down the canyon and waterfalls lasting about 4 hours

Notes: In accordance with weather conditions, the program is subject to minor changes.
