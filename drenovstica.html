Canyon Drenoštica

Explore Drenoštica

5+ 3h 1.2km 80€

Drenoštica Canyon is located in the hills above Budva, which are only 15 minutes from the city. The canyon is perfect for people new to canyoning. It is decorated with beautiful nature several jumps, slides and abseils. Sunny along its entire length, it has a moderate water temperature that it never dries up.


PROGRAMME

• Gathering in Budva in the morning
• Drive 20 minutes to the entrance to the
canyon • Getting to know the equipment and the type of planned activities.
• Canyoning - descent down the canyon and waterfalls for about 3 hours.
• Leaving the canyon and walking for 20 minutes to the car where the activity ends

Notes: In accordance with weather conditions, the program is subject to minor changes.

DETAILS

The price is 80 euros per person
For groups of 8+ people, we grant additional discounts

The price includes:
• diving suit and diving socks
• helmet
• canyoning belt
• rope and other accompanying technical equipment
• canyoning guides
• taxes
• photos
The price does not include:
• transportation
• lunch

Conditions

The age of team participants must not be less than 12 years or more than 60.
Minor participants must be accompanied by a parent
Students must know how to swim
The participant must be a healthy individual
Consumption of alcohol before passing through the canyon is prohibited
You need to bring a swimsuit, shorts and dry clothes with you.
