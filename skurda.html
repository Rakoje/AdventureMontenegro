Škurda Canyon

Explore Škurda

3+ 4h 1km 100€

The Škurda canyon is located above Kotor, and it belongs to the vertical canyons. It is composed of about 20 vertical heights from 4 to 27 meters. This canyon has been without water since the beginning of July. The length is approx 1200 meters with a vertical difference of 300m. Due to its length, the canyon is divided into three parts i the third, which is also the most attractive, is used for passing.

PROGRAMME

• Gathering in Kotor in the morning
• Getting to know the equipment and the type of planned activities.
• Walking for 30 minutes along the serpentines to the entrance to the
canyon • Canyoning - descent down the canyon and waterfalls for about 4 hours.
• Exiting the canyon located in Kotor

Notes: In accordance with weather conditions, the program is subject to minor changes

Conditions

The age of team participants must not be less than 12 years or more than 60.
Minor participants must be accompanied by a parent
Students must know how to swim
The participant must be a healthy individual
Consumption of alcohol before passing through the canyon is prohibited
You need to bring a swimsuit, shorts and dry clothes with you.
