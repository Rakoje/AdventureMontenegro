Canyon Međureč

Explore Međureč

3+ 4h 1.5km 100€

Only half an hour's drive from the center of Bar is one of the most attractive canyons in Montenegro, a paradise for adventurers - the Međureč canyon. It is located on the slopes of Mount Rumija, just a few kilometers from the coast of the Adriatic Sea and about 15 km from the port of Bar. Although it is relatively short (less than 2 km), due to the large amount of water (even in the summer months), beautiful verticals and pools, this canyon is one of the top canyoning destinations in Montenegro.


PROGRAM

• Gathering in the Bar at the parking lot of the Laković hypermarket in the morning
• Drive 20 minutes to the foot of the
canyon • Getting to know the equipment and the type of planned activities.
• Transport by all-terrain vehicle to the starting position
• A 10-minute walk down the mountain path to the entrance to the
canyon • Canyoning - descent down the canyon and waterfalls lasting about 4 hours
• Walking 10 minutes to the vehicle where the activity ends

Notes: In accordance with weather conditions, the program is subject to minor changes.

DETAILS

The price is 100 euros per person
For groups of 8+ people, we grant additional discounts

The price includes:
• diving suit and diving socks
• helmet
• canyoning belt
• rope and other accompanying technical equipment
• canyoning guides
• taxes
• photos
The price does not include:
• transportation
• lunch

Conditions

The age of team participants must not be less than 12 years or more than 60.
Minor participants must be accompanied by a parent
Students must know how to swim
The participant must be a healthy individual
Consumption of alcohol before passing through the canyon is prohibited
You need to bring a swimsuit, shorts and dry clothes with you.
